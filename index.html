What We Offer

Comprehensive Dental Services

From your first cleaning to a complete smile transformation, we provide care at every stage.

Preventive Care

  • Exams & Cleanings — Thorough oral health assessments and gentle professional cleanings
  • X-Rays — Low-radiation digital imaging for accurate, early diagnosis

Restorative Dentistry

  • Fillings — Durable, tooth-colored composite fillings
  • Crowns & Bridges — Custom restorations to protect or replace damaged teeth
  • Partial & Full Dentures — Comfortable, natural-looking solutions for missing teeth

Cosmetic Enhancements

  • Cosmetic Bonding — Minimally invasive repair of chips, gaps, and discoloration
  • Veneers — Ultra-thin porcelain shells for a brilliant, natural-looking smile

Specialty Appliances

  • Bruxism Night Guard — Custom-fitted guards to protect against teeth grinding and jaw discomfort

Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Fran Bihary, DMD

Dr. Bihary is a lifelong Kinnelon resident with deep roots in this community. Her practice has been built on a simple belief: every patient deserves attentive, personalized care delivered in a comfortable, judgment-free environment.

She stays current with the latest advances in dental techniques and technology through ongoing professional education, ensuring that her patients always have access to modern, evidence-based care.

Whether you're coming in for a routine cleaning or a complete smile makeover, you'll find that Dr. Bihary takes the time to listen, explain your options clearly, and make you feel at home.

First Visit?

New Patient Information

We want your first visit to be smooth and stress-free. Here's what to expect.

01

Before Your Visit

  • Bring your photo ID and insurance card
  • Arrive 10–15 minutes early to complete paperwork
  • List any current medications you're taking
  • Note any dental concerns or questions you'd like to discuss
02

Your First Appointment

  • Comprehensive oral health exam
  • Digital X-rays as needed
  • Professional cleaning (if time allows)
  • Personalized treatment plan discussion
03

  • We'll verify and explain your benefits
  • Flexible payment options available
